YouTube videos use a base 64 system for each of the 11 digits of their entire ID.

a-z, A-Z, 0-9, and - & _ are the digits used for that base 64 system.

This means there are 64^11 possible video IDs, most are not used yet.

Very often you'll get a link that leads to a nonexistant video. Just retry!

By default, this project won't open up a new tab with the generated YouTube link.

You can click the colored button to toggle if you want it to open a new tab.
